How to Ride the Bus

SFMTA Transit

Finding your stop

Plan your trip using 511 Transit trip planner

Arrive early. Buses have schedules and they will not wait for you.Make sure the bus driver can see you. That bench down the street may be tempting to rest at, but wait at your stop. Don't stand too close to the street.

Payment

Visitor Passes - 1, 3, or 7 consecutive days View the map of locations here: https://www.clippercard.com/ClipperWeb/map.do Keep your fare receipt/transfer at all times. Valid up to 90 minutes after purchase to transfer on other buses

Getting on the bus

Have exact payment ready (cash, clipper card) Wait until all exiting passengers are off the bus before climbing on. You can enter in the front or rear doors with a clipper card, or only through the front door with cash. If you are young, keep moving to the back of the bus. Sit down and enjoy the ride!

Exiting the bus

Pull the yellow stop cord before your stop. Gather all of your belongings. Wait until the bus comes to a complete stop, thank your bus driver, and then exit the bus. Finish your walk to your destination, and have fun!
